Frequently Asked Questions

I want to delete my account. How do I do it?

Click on the profile icon on the top right side of the screen and click on Delete my account.

Can I choose COD as my preferred mode of payement?

Yes, you can. When you are asked to select the mode of payement, there is an option of COD. You can choose that.

Is there a way to change my contact details when I am placing an order?

Yes, you can do that by clicking on the profile icon on the top right corner of the screen and selecting edit my profile.

Where can I track my order status?

In the profile section on the top right corner of the screen, there is a section of Order History. You can track your order there.

Can I change the language for viewing the website?

No, this feature is not yet available. But, we are working on it.
